Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Children aged 3-6 years are in the preschool or kindergarten stage, marked by rapid development in language, social skills, and motor abilities.

Elementary School

It is a time when students develop critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and a deeper understanding of the world around them.

Middle (10-16) Years

The middle years, between 10 and 16, are a period of significant physical, emotional, and cognitive growth as children transition into adolescence.
